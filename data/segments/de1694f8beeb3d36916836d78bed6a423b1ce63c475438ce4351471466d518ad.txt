**Necessary conditions:**
1. **Presupposes knowledge** — it can only be exercised by an agent that possesses relevant knowledge; wisdom without knowledge is uninformed judgment.
2. **Evaluative** — it involves assessing goals, values, and consequences, not merely deriving true conclusions.
3. **Context-sensitive** — the same knowledge may be applied differently in different situations; wisdom is the capacity to determine the appropriate application.
4. **Non-algorithmic** — it cannot be fully reduced to a procedure; it requires judgment in the face of incomplete specifications and trade-offs.
