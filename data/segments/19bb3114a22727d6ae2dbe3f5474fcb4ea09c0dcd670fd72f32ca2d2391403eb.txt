- Question: how should capability debt be defined, measured, and used as a leading indicator of AI-related enterprise risk, and how does pre-existing debt amplify risk once autonomous, goal-directed AI systems remove human rate limits?
- Scope: organisational debt in process quality, review quality, skill inventory, governance, and workaround behaviour, not source-code debt in isolation, vendor selection, or macroeconomic adoption modelling.
- Constraints: public and accessible sources first, explicit separation of fact from inference, acronym expansion on first use, and prior repository items treated as adjacent synthesis rather than sole evidence.
- Output: knowledge.
