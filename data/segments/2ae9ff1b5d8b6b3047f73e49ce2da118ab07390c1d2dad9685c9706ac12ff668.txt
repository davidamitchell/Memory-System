**Necessary conditions:**
1. **Finite** — the collection has defined membership; there is a fact of the matter about whether any given document is in or out.
2. **Purposive** — assembled with an explicit analytical or representational goal, not arbitrarily accumulated.
3. **Document-constituted** — composed exclusively of documents (discrete recorded-knowledge units).
4. **Domain-relevant** — all member documents pertain to the domain under study.
