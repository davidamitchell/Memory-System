**Necessary conditions:**
1. **Recurrent** — it appears across multiple documents or assertions; it is not singular or isolated.
2. **Identifiable** — it can be named and its scope described, even if its boundaries are approximate.
3. **Semantic** — it is defined by meaning-proximity, not by syntactic co-occurrence alone.
4. **Sub-formal** — it does not impose necessary and sufficient conditions on its members; membership is a matter of degree or judgment.
